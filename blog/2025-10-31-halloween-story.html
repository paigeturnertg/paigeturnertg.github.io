Halloween Story!

It's my first Halloween since I started writing fiction, so of course I was going to try my hand at the classic costume-gone-wrong tale.

I'm excited to share Hex Lives of College Girls! It's a light, trope-filled romp that I had so much fun writing and I hope everyone enjoys reading it! It was a lot of fun to try a different style of illustration—hand-drawn pulp fiction/graphic novel style seemed like a great fit for the subject matter. Let me know what you think!
