The Hex Trilogy Concludes

Coming this Valentine's Day, 2026!

Kylie and Colette have survived Halloween and Christmas. They've been transformed, diminished, carved away piece by piece. They've lost their bodies, their minds, and very nearly themselves.

And now, on Valentine's Day, they're offered a choice: salvation, or each other.

Hexes and Woes publishes this Saturday, February 14th—the conclusion to the trilogy that started with Hex Lives of College Girls and continued through Hex the Halls.

This one's different. No mysterious shopkeepers, no cursed objects. Just two broken people, an impossible choice, and the question of whether love can exist when you've forgotten how to trust.

If you're new to the series, you can start with Part 1: "Hex Lives of College Girls and Part 2: "Hex the Halls". If you've been following Kyle and Cole—now Kylie and Colette—from the beginning, Saturday is when you finally (maybe) get closure.

The prisoner's dilemma has never been this personal.

See you on Valentine's Day!

—Paige
