A Little About Me

✦

I’m Paige Turner—and yes, the pun is terrible, but I simply couldn’t help myself. I’ve been marinating in this genre since the early 2000s, lurking in the stacks long enough to see every trope and cliché imaginable. After decades of watching from the sidelines, I finally decided it was time to pick up the pen and try writing something myself.

I’m obsessed with the psychology of the "no-win" situation: the moment when a protagonist realizes that every "logical" choice they made has led them somewhere they can never leave. The realization that there will be no going back? Priceless.
