A Little About Me

✦

I'm Paige Turner—and yes, the name is absolutely intentional. I write transformation fiction that blends heart, humor, and just a touch of chaos. My stories explore what it means to change and become someone wonderfully new.

I believe the best transformation fiction doesn't just describe change—it makes you feel it. That flutter of possibility. That moment when everything shifts.
