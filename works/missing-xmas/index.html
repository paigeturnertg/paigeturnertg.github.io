Miss-ing You This Christmas

Short Story

#Magical #Seasonal #Sweet/Romantic

Big-city journalist Mark Holly arrives in Pine Hollow to research an article about small-town Christmas. But one borrowed Christmas sweater, one registration mix-up, and one widowed tree farmer later, he finds he’s living a Hallmark movie come to life. Along the way, Mark learns that sometimes the story you end up writing isn’t always the one you were looking for.
